Anomaly Explanation Interface

Guide to Use:

1) After identifying an anomaly, use this section to explore potential phases where it might have occurred.

2) The system will analyze historical data to highlight possible reasons for the anomaly.

3) Hit the "Analyze" button below to start the process, and the table will be updated with results.

4) Each entry in the table relates to unusual readings detected in a specific cycle stage.

5) The sensor and robot details pinpoint the exact source of the abnormal readings.

6) The 'Cycle State' and 'Function' columns specify where in the process the anomaly was detected.
